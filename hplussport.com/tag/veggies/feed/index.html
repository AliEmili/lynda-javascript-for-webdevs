However, the truth is, eating five or more servings of fruit and vegetables a day is still your best bet for getting optimal nutrition. In addition, eating your veggies won’t break the bank, like so many vitamin products can. If after eating real food for optimum nutrition, you still want to take a supplement, know that all supplements are not created equal. Select only vitamins that have been evaluated by a third party laboratory to ensure the contents in the bottle match the claims on the label.
